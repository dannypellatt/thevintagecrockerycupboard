About Us


Welcome to The Vintage Crockery Cupboard! Our adventure began in 2022 when we were tasked with sourcing unique vintage crockery for our son's wedding. Unable to find affordable and local options, we turned our dilemma into an opportunity: if we couldn't hire it, we'd create it ourselves! Thus, our passion project was born.

Driven by our mission to infuse traditional charm into events, we're not just a hiring service, we also sell our unique items. We take pride in our handcrafted three-tier cake stands. Whether created from our hand-selected plates or from cherished plates you bring us, we promise a personalised touch.

Why choose us? We believe in affordability without compromising on quality. Every piece in our collection tells a story, as they are all vintage and picked with care. Our commitment is to make events memorable and magical, with a touch of eco-consciousness. By upcycling good-quality plates, cups, and saucers, we breathe new life into beautiful crockery.

At our core, we're a family-run venture, lovingly led by Sue. Our ties with the community extend beyond business, as we champion local charities, sourcing all our vintage items from them. Find us at local craft fairs, or get in touch to experience the charm. We're here to make your event a little more magical, a tad more nostalgic, and deeply memorable.
